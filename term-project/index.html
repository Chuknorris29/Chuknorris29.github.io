Welcome to The Mountain Spoke Home Page!

Since 1950, The Mountain Spoke has been providing excellent products and services to bicycle enthusiasts in Bozeman, Montana and surrounding areas. Over the years, The Mountain Spoke has achieved a reputation of quality that is unparalleled. This has lead many bike professionals and hobbyists from around the world to come to The Mountain Spoke for products, repairs, and training.

Whether you are an extreme bike fanatic, or just getting started, The Mountain Spoke has got you covered. We offer the latest and greatest in bicycle technology to give you the edge you need to reach the level you crave. Already have a bike but need some repairs? We can handle that, too!

Not quite ready for the whole 100 mile endurance race, but still want to get outside and enjoy a ride you wont forget? Then check out our adventure tours page in the navigation. We have tours daily that will not only be fun, but help get you to the next level in racing performance. Looking for a world class race that will test your capabilities to their absolute limit?
