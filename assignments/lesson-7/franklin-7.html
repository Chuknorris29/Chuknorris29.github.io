Rain Saves Tulips

I planted my Tulips in the spring, like every good gardener should. It required work, but I loved the exercise. The Tulips seemed to be doing well. However, we had been going through a dry spell with no rain in sight, and I had to leave on a business trip for a week. Who would stay and water my tulips? I hadn't a clue. My family lived far away, and my neighbors were stand-offish. So, I prayed that my Tulips would be protected and watered while I was gone, hoping for a miracle.

During my trip, I checked the weather everyday. The forecast looked grim. I thought my tulips were doomed. Then, one morning I woke up, and quickly pulled up Accurate Weather on my phone. To my great surpirse, it had rained over night! It was a miracle!
