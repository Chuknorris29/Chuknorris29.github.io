Adventure Tours

What are the Adventure Tours?

The Adventure Tours are professionally guided tours designed for people of all skill levels. They are held twice daily, and take you through some of the most beautiful bike paths of the Bozeman area.

Bridger Mountains Trail Tour

This tour takes the participants on a journy through the famous Bridger Mountains Trail 534. The elevation gain can reach 2,761 feet, yikes!

  • Meet-up Time: 6:00AM
  • Skill Level: Moderate-Difficult
  • Length: 6.7 Miles
  • Location: Bridger Mountain Trail
  • Cost: $10.00 Per Person

Sourdough Ridge Loop Tour

This tour takes the participants through the Sourdough Rdige Loop trail. The distance is shorter, and the inclines are not as extreme as the Bridger Mountains Trail Tour. We recommend this trail for people who are getting started in the sport.

  • Meet-up Time: 7:00PM
  • Skill Level: Easy-Moderate
  • Length: 1.9 Miles
  • Location: Sourdough Ridge Loop
  • Cost: $10.00 Per Person
