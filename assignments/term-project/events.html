Race Events

Cycle The Continental Divide!

Looking for a bike race event that you will never forget? You found it. The Continental Divide race is an event like no other. You and hundreds of other people will come together for an annual event that takes you through a biking experience that will test you to your limits.

  • Date: July 23th-27th
  • Distance: Approximately 24 Miles
  • Cost: $100.00 Per Person

The bike route starts at the Bridger Mountains Trail, then cuts into Sypse Canyon Trail. The bike route ends back at the Bridger Mountains Trail starting point.

Vendors will be setup at the event start/end location, but be prepared to pay for food and beverages. Participants are expected to attain thair own lodging, if required. However, contact us if you would like a recommendation!

Participants must be 18 years of age or older and must be willing to consent to a medical release form.
